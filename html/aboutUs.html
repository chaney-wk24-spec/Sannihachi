    Make the chat more FUN!

    Our company founded in 2025, Sannihachi is dedicated to help people to make conversation more lively and engaging. Our vision is to bring emotions to life through fun, expressive and engaging emojis, building a digital world filled with humor and creativity— because our brand and audience deserve happiness.
